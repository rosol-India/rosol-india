INSTALLATION AND COMMISSIONING

We design, install and maintain cost-effective solar energy systems. The company provides customer focus and support while saving custumers' money and protecting the environment.
We offer turnkey installation and commissioning of solar plants in India. We have installed rooftop and ground mounted solar plants in many sites. We have a dealer network that covers all of Delhi through whom we can provide local installation and support for your solar projects.


Our Installation and Commissioning services include
  • Material Handling
  • Site Preparation
  • Structure Mounting
  • Module Mounting
  • Cabling and Interconnection
  • Inverter Erection and Installation
  • Execution as per design with proper methodology
  • Interconnection to grid power
  • System performance testing
  • Projects completed on schedule
